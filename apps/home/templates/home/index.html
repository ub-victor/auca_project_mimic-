About AUCA

Built on Faith.
Driven by Purpose.

AUCA is a Seventh-day Adventist institution in Kigali, Rwanda, dedicated to holistic education. We blend academic rigor with Christian values to produce graduates who lead with integrity across Africa and beyond.

Faith-Centered

Grounded in Adventist values of integrity, service, and wholeness.

Accredited

Internationally recognized qualifications across all disciplines.

Pan-African

Graduates serving communities across the African continent.

Community

A vibrant campus life across three campuses in Rwanda.
